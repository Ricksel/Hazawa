Check out my Topaz and Numby

    Topaz and Numby is a 5 star character from Honkai: Star Rail that follows the path of "The Hunt", a path
    that deals a massive amount of damage to a single enemy. Topaz and Numby gameplay are focused on follow up
    that triggers if a certain conditions are met. To trigger the follow up attacks of Numby, a character from
    your team must deal a follow up attack damage to the enemy that have been marked by Topaz also all of Topaz's
    attacks are considered as follow up attacks.






    Here is the picture of Topaz and Numby, because why not? they both cute. By the way Topaz is the white haired
    girl and Numby is her pet a golden Swine-like creature called Trotter.






    Here is the video clip of how Topaz and Numby defeated the Doomsday Beast together with March 7th, Clara, and Huohuo.

    was overshadowed by Topaz and Numby hehe.
