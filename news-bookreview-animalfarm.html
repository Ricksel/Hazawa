Book Review: "Animal Farm" George Orwell

    It is a novel written by George Orwell about what if the animals in a farm thinks like and behave like a human.
    Animal Farm for me is a story that represents the dark side of our society because I discovered while reading this
    that each of the animals represents something. In the story I have encountered Napoleon a pig and also a dictator in the
    story. Napoleon abused his power and brought fear to all of the animals in the farm, Napoleon also ordered his adoptive
    dogs to execute anyone who opposed him, by executing means killing them.


    There are also a lot of noteworthy characters in the story like Snowball a pig same with Napoleon, he used to be a leader
    in the farm before Napoleon overthrown him. I like his character he is so cool and he acts alone like a cool main character in
    an anime. Who would have thought that there are cool characters from a story that consist mostly domestic animals. Also there's
    Boxer an old and strong horse, I like Boxer because of his behavior, I wanted to be like him he is humble, a man with few words
    and also a workaholic character in the story he is the strongest among the rest but he did not use his strength to assert dominance
    instead he used his strength to help the weak.


    Overall, the story was great I love the story and I do not mind to read it again because it is so good. Animal Farm by George Orwell
    is a must read and I recommend it to anyone who loves reading books, novel, classic literature like me.
