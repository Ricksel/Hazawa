Childe is the best character
on this g**damn game

    Childe, Tartaglia, Ajax, Ed Sheeran or whatever the community named him is a character from Genshin Impact.
    He is a Bow wielding character from the Nation of Schneznaya, and Hydro vision holder which means his power
    is related to water. Childe's playstyle is quite unique, he is a bow wielding character but he can change stance
    and start beating his opponents with his dual daggers made of water.



    Here is the clip of Tartaglia/Childe gameplay with his team consist of Kazuha, Bennett and Mona. Childe is a
    a strong unit but with a help of these 3 support characters he will become a lot stronger. Kazuha is responsible
    for defense shredding and boosting elemental damage, Bennett is responsible for healing and give attack bonuses
    and lastly, Mona is responsible for giving the enemies a status that will make any damage they will receive a lot stronger.



    Bennett is a sword-wielding character and a Pyro vision.


    Mona is a catalyst-wielding character and also a Hydro vision holder like Childe.



    Kaedehara Kazuha, also a sword-wielding character and an Anemo vision holder.
